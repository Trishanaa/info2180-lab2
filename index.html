First Blog Post

My name is Trishana, and I'm a 20-year-old university student in my second year, working towards my goal of becoming a cybersecurity analyst. I've always been fascinated by technology and the idea of protecting systems from cyber threats. When I'm not studying, I love to read books that take me on new adventures and unwind with action movies that keep me hooked. This semester, I'm really excited to dive into web development. I can't wait to expand my skills and learn how to build and secure websites, which I know will be an important part of my future in cybersecurity.

Second Blog Post

October 1, 2024 by Trishana

Alongside my academic journey, I’m constantly seeking out new challenges that help me grow, both personally and professionally. I believe that being a cybersecurity analyst isn’t just about protecting networks; it’s about understanding how everything works, from code to user behavior. That’s why I’m particularly thrilled to start web development this semester—it feels like another step toward becoming a more well-rounded tech professional. I’ve always enjoyed solving complex problems, and web development will give me a hands-on experience with the digital spaces I’ll eventually be defending. It’s exciting to think about how these skills will come together to shape my future career.
